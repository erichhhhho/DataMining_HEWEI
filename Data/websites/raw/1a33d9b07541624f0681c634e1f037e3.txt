<h4 class="artikel">Sports</h4>
<h1>Systematic doping of Chinese athletes in Olympic Games revealed by former doctor</h1>

<p class="intro">A former doctor has revealed the massive extent of doping of Chinese Olympic athletes during the 1980s and 1990s. The whistleblower has claimed more than 10,000 athletes were doped in the state-backed program. </p>

<div class="picBox full">
<a class="overlayLink" href="#"
link="/overlay/image/article/41065227/41065243" rel="nofollow"
style="cursor: pointer;">
<img src="/image/41065243_303.jpg" title="The Chinese team at the 1984 Los Angeles Olympic Games" alt="The Chinese team at the 1984 Los Angeles Olympic Games" /> </a>
<p>The Chinese team at the 1984 Los Angeles Olympic Games</p>
</div>

<div class="group">
<div class="longText">
<p>A former doctor for the Chinese Olympic team told German media that tens of thousands of <a href="/en/china-faces-weightlifting-ban-after-three-athletes-are-stripped-of-beijing-gold/a-37115221">Chinese athletes</a> took performance enhancing drugs in the 1980s and 1990s as part of a systematic government doping scheme.</p> <p>"There must have been more than 10,000 people involved," Xue Yinxian told public broadcaster ARD in a television interview first broadcast on Friday. "All international medals (won by Chinese athletes in that time) should be taken back."</p> <p>Xue's claim of systematic doping contradicted previous statements by <a href="/en/flawed-stars-on-chinese-flags-at-olympics-causes-a-stir/a-19458319">the Chinese government</a>, which had denied any involvement in individual cases of Chinese athletes taking performance enhancing drugs.</p> <p><em>Read more:</em> <a href="/en/iditarod-dog-sled-team-tests-positive-for-tramadol-in-drug-doping-test/a-41033862">Iditarod dog sled team tests positive for Tramadol in drug doping test</a></p> <div class="picBox	medium
