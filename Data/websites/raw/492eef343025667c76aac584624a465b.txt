        <div class="body-copy">
            <p>Overwhelmed by the three-hours-plus <a itemscope="itemscope" itemprop="StoryLink" href="https://www.bloomberg.com/politics/articles/2017-10-18/xi-imagines-china-in-2050-highlights-from-his-three-hour-speech" title="Xi Imagines China in 2050: Highlights From His Three-Hour Speech" target="_blank"><meta itemprop="active" content="true"><meta itemprop="type" content="StoryLink"><meta itemprop="suid" content="OY0B346JIJVA">vision statement</a> served up by Xi Jinping today? A quick word search reveals a lot.</p><div class="regwall" data-position="1"></div><p>Bloomberg Intelligence crunched the rhetoric in the Chinese-language version of the president&#x2019;s speech to the 19th Communist Party congress. The results are emblematic of China&#x2019;s bid to pivot away from the smokestack industries that delivered it double-digit growth as public dissatisfaction over pollution and <a itemscope="itemscope" itemprop="StoryLink" href="https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2017-08-06/spies-blockchain-and-alibaba-beating-china-s-fake-food-scourge" title="Battle Against China&#x2019;s Fake Foods Drives New Tech Frontier (1)" target="_blank"><meta itemprop="active" content="true"><meta itemprop="type" content="StoryLink"><meta itemprop="suid" content="OUAY2W6JTSG5">food scandals</a> mounts.</p><div class="regwall" data-position="2"></div><p>Xi used the word &#x201C;environment&#x201D; and other related terms 89 times in Wednesday&#x2019;s monologue, while his predecessor Hu Jintao mentioned it 74 times in his 2012 address, according to the <a itemscope="itemscope" itemprop="StoryLink" href="/news/terminal/OY0H826TTDS0" title="China Party Congress Word Count Shows Xi&#x2019;s China Dream Is Green" class="terminal-news-story" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener"><meta itemprop="active" content="true"><meta itemprop="type" content="StoryLink"><meta itemprop="suid" content="OY0H826TTDS0">BI analysis</a>. Reference to the &#x201C;economy,&#x201D; meanwhile, dropped to 70 from a hefty 104 five years ago.</p><div class="regwall" data-position="3"></div><figure data-type="image" data-id="319309994" data-image-type="chart" data-image-size="full" data-align="center" data-chart-type="line" style="max-width: 1200px;" class="figure-expandable"><div class="image"><div id="lazy-img-319309994" class="lazy-img"><style>#lazy-img-319309994:before{padding-top:57.41666666666667%;}</style><img src="https://assets.bwbx.io/images/users/iqjWHBFdfxIU/iR_NCi4b6st8/v1/60x-1.png" data-native-src="https://assets.bwbx.io/images/users/iqjWHBFdfxIU/iR_NCi4b6st8/v1/-1x-1.png" class="lazy-img__image"></div></div></figure>
        <div id="box-fsv6859" class="page-ad" data-position="mobile-box">
