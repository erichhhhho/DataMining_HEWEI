<p>The hashtag <a href="https://www.weibo.com/p/100808a1c67b986ee04d770f8c5d9d9f6b5468">#å½­äºæåºæ#</a> "Eddie Peng comes out" has literally lit up the Chinese internet. Juicy rumors are swirling around social media that the Taiwanese-Canadian star and Zhang Lei, founder and CEO of Hillhouse Capital Group, are now an item.</p>

<p>Eddie Peng (å½­äºæ), a 35-year-old singer-turned-actor is <a href="http://chinafilminsider.com/why-monkey-king-eddie-peng-is-one-of-asias-most-prolific-performers/">one of Asia's biggest and most beloved stars</a>, headlining in a series of blockbuster action films in recent years that have turned him into one of the continent's most sought-after bachelors. Over the years, the heartthrob has been linked to a number of alleged girlfriends, including <a href="http://asianpopnews.com/eddie-peng-denies-dating-dada-chan/">Chinese bombshell Dada Chan</a> and <a href="http://asianfanaticfans.blogspot.com/2008/11/eddie-peng-says-hes-not-in-love.html">Mandopop diva Jolin Tsai </a>, but has <a href="http://www.chinatopix.com/articles/60953/20150807/eddie-peng-hopes-to-marry-soon-rumored-to-have-bought-elite-apartment.htm">mostly managed</a> to keep his private life a secret.</p>

<p>Meanwhile, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhang_Lei_(investor)">Zhang Lei</a> (å¼ ç£), 45, is one of China's richest investors, founding Hillhouse Capital in 2005 with a $20 million investment. Now, the group reportedly manages over $20 billion. Despite his wealth, Zhang <a href="https://www.nytimes.com/2015/04/03/business/the-chinese-billionaire-zhang-lei-spins-research-into-investment-gold.html">likes to live frugally</a> and stay out of the eye of the press.</p>
