class="field-item even"><p>As it has already been said in some previous articles, there is no need to explain what WeChat is. Indeed, according to the latest news, WeChat will soon reach 900 million users.</p><p>In this week’s article, you will find some interesting key points about WeChat and more precisely some indications to help you to create and develop a viral post.</p><h2>What does it mean to become viral?</h2><p>By becoming viral that means you are using some viral marketing and this is a marketing technique that uses social network and other technologies to increase brand awareness or to achieve some other marketing objectives (either a long term objective such as the brand awareness or a short term objective such as the promotion of a specific product). The idea here is to spread news or messages as fast as possible and it is often done by word of mouth .</p><p>Regarding WeChat, it can be a great place and tool to be able to do some viral marketing. Indeed, WeChat has a huge potential thanks to its number of daily users. Moreover, if you refer to one of the previous articles, you will see that WeChat has a great user retention rate. <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/qpsoftware-ces-asia-shanghai-2017-jason-blondeau" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(140, 104, 203); text-decoration-line: none; background: transparent; outline: none; cursor: pointer; word-wrap: break-word;" target="_blank">They use it often during the day and mainly for personal use</a>.</p><h2>How can you create a successful viral post?</h2><p>First of all there are different ways to create a successful viral post. We could categorize them in two categories: the creation of viral posts through the use of different tools  such as emails, social networks, etc; and the other one would be to create an exceptional content that will stimulate a big interest to your audience.</p><p>There are different ways to raise your users' interest:</p><ul><li><strong>Make it visual:</strong> it sounds like an evidence but if you try to post a video on WeChat for instance, it would be a great way to make it visual.</li><li><strong>Stimulate emotions</strong>: post can resonate with the feelings of your users. They will feel the content and the message will be forwarded/shared to generate more views.</li><li><strong>Keep the campaign simple</strong>: “A picture is worth a thousand words” this idiom is the best explanation possible. Keep it simple and easy to understand otherwise you will lose the interest of your users.</li><li><strong>Be controversial</strong>: this is a bit tricky in China, you need to respect the rules of the country but if you start being controversial you will gather the attention of people and will create a wide-range discussion with people having different opinions.</li><li><strong>Generate curiosity</strong>: curiosity is the core of all innovation. If you can start playing with that you will catch your users' interest since they will want to know the crux of the matter.</li><li><strong>Be interactive</strong>: the more creative you are, the more the people will have interest in your post. Also if you combine your creativity with the possibility to create something that allows people to have an interaction either through a game or anything else, the interest will be much higher and you will be able to engage with more people.</li></ul><h2>What to do before launching a viral campaign?</h2><p>If you want to have a successful viral post/campaign you should be aware of some points. You cannot launch something like this without any precaution. You should keep these few remarks in mind:</p><ul><li><strong>Define your objectives</strong>: it is really important to know what you want to achieve before launching your campaign. If you did not realize that part you will not be able to establish some KPIs and see if your work was successful.</li><li><strong>Know your audience</strong>: if you want to launch a campaign and target everyone, your chance of success will not be very favorable. That is why you should know who you want to target and know what is the best way to reach to them.</li><li><strong>Plan your message</strong>: this point is related to the previous part of the article. You have to plan and prepare what you will tell to your audience. It is best to prepare yourself and understand what will work on your target.</li></ul><p><strong>What is your next step?</strong></p><p>This article is here to help and give you some ideas. Of course, every concept and every project is different that is why you should adapt it to your own needs and objectives but reading this article will point you towards the right direction.
