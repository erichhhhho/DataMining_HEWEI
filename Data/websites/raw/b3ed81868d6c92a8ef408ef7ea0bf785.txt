    <div class="article-body">
        <p>China's Communist Party began its twice-a-decade congress on Oct. 18 in Beijing as over 2,000 representatives from around the country gathered.</p>
<p>During the week-long major political event through Oct. 24, the party decides on its long-term policies, makes amendments to the party's constitution and chooses members to key party positions.&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://img.kyodonews.net/english/public/images/posts/48bc125d1755a5e2d65a4b465f1c32d8/photo_l.jpg" width="100%" /></p>
<p>The 19th National Congress is held at Beijing's Great Hall of the People.</p>
<p><img src="https://img.kyodonews.net/english/public/images/posts/3488e8823c7c57ae6615ec957f3a643e/photo_l.jpg" width="100%" /></p>
<p>About 2,200 representatives selected among the nation's 89 million communist party members take part in the congress.</p>
<p><img src="https://img.kyodonews.net/english/public/images/posts/f498ce5b3717249af9bbf2c3c67e03ec/photo_l.jpg" width="100%" /></p>
<p>Some 200 will be newly elected to the party's central committee during the event.</p>
<p><img src="https://img.kyodonews.net/english/public/images/posts/10b5c1fc0dfeefe30f091c68c2213cf3/photo_l.jpg" width="100%" /></p>
<p>Participants include representatives of ethnic minority groups.&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://img.kyodonews.net/english/public/images/posts/dc3267d6dd1b970fbb2c1aa5911562d8/photo_l.jpg" width="100%" /></p>
<p>Security has been tightened in the city ahead of the meeting amid fears of potential protest demonstrations. China faces persistent criticisms for suppressing democracy campaigners and ethnic minorities.</p>
<p><img src="https://img.kyodonews.net/english/public/images/posts/acc1ca2967dee8971acabed5ae584ecd/photo_l.jpg" width="100%" /></p>
<p>A banner urging people to unite around China's President Xi Jinping has been posted in the city.</p>
<p><img src="https://img.kyodonews.net/english/public/images/posts/83adea89848a35e4c39b4a5f358c5458/photo_l.jpg" width="100%" /></p>
<p>Xi Jinping delivers a political report at the congress for the first time. The speech in 2012 congress was made by his predecessor Hu Jintao.</p>
<p><img src="https://img.kyodonews.net/english/public/images/posts/0aa56e32131d8e98ac785b3afab7e83a/photo_l.jpg" width="100%" /></p>
<p>China's national flag is hoisted in Tiananmen Square.</p>
<p><img src="https://img.kyodonews.net/english/public/images/posts/4aa8c265b163577e87c813bdc83f806e/photo_l.jpg" width="100%" /></p>
<p>Attendees sing the national anthem at the congress.</p>
